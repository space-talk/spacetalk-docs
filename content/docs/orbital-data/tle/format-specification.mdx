# TLE Format Specification

TLEs consist of two lines, each 69 characters long (excluding CR/LF or LF newline). Columns are 1-based. Fields are right-justified unless noted.

Implied decimals mean no explicit '.'; parse by inserting before the digits (e.g., '0005770' → 0.0005770).

Units are as specified; angles in degrees.

## Line 1

### Line 1 - Record 1: Line Number

**Field**: Line Number

**Formal Name and Purpose**: Identifies as Line 1. Must be '1' for structural integrity.

**Line**: 1

**Columns (1-based, inclusive)**: 1-1

**Width**: 1

**Type**: Char

**Sign Rules**: N/A

**Implied Decimal**: No

**Units**: N/A

**Valid Range**: '1' only

**Typical Range**: '1'

**Dependencies/Interactions**: Must match '2' on Line 2; mismatch rejects pair.

**Example Values (incl. Edges)**: '1' (normal); blank (reject).

**Notes**: Shall reject if not '1'. Informs no semantics beyond structure.

---

### Line 1 - Record 2: Satellite Catalog Number

**Field**: Satellite Catalog Number

**Formal Name and Purpose**: NORAD satellite ID (unique integer). Used for identification and matching across lines.

**Line**: 1

**Columns (1-based, inclusive)**: 3-7

**Width**: 5

**Type**: Integer

**Sign Rules**: Unsigned

**Implied Decimal**: No

**Units**: Unitless

**Valid Range**: 1 - 99999

**Typical Range**: 1 - 50000+

**Dependencies/Interactions**: Must match Line 2; used by SGP4 for ID only (not propagation). Future > 5 digits: parse as string, warn.

**Example Values (incl. Edges)**: '00005' (Vanguard 1); '99999' (edge); blank (parse as 0, warn).

**Notes**: Right-padded with spaces if < 5 digits. Shall reject mismatch with Line 2.

---

### Line 1 - Record 3: Classification

**Field**: Classification

**Formal Name and Purpose**: Security classification. Used for access control; irrelevant to orbital mechanics.

**Line**: 1

**Columns (1-based, inclusive)**: 8-8

**Width**: 1

**Type**: Char

**Sign Rules**: N/A

**Implied Decimal**: No

**Units**: N/A

**Valid Range**: 'U', 'C', 'S' (unclassified, classified, secret); STR#3 also allows blank.

**Typical Range**: 'U' (public), blank (common).

**Dependencies/Interactions**: None. May affect data distribution but not propagation.

**Example Values (incl. Edges)**: 'U' (unclassified); blank (unknown/legacy).

**Notes**: Public TLEs typically 'U' or blank. Parse leniently; non-standard chars acceptable with warnings.

---

### Line 1 - Record 4: International Launch Designator

**Field**: International Launch Designator

**Formal Name and Purpose**: Launch year, launch of year, and piece ID (YYYY-NNN[letter(s)]). Links to launch event.

**Line**: 1

**Columns (1-based, inclusive)**: 10-17

**Width**: 8

**Type**: String

**Sign Rules**: N/A

**Implied Decimal**: No

**Units**: N/A

**Valid Range**: Format YYLLLPPP where YY=year (00–99), LLL=launch number (001-999), PPP=piece (A–ZZZ).

**Typical Range**: 57A01 (Sputnik 1, 1957) to present.

**Dependencies/Interactions**: May help cross-reference but not used by SGP4. Year ambiguity: apply windowing (e.g., 57-99 -> 19XX, 00-56 -> 20XX).

**Example Values (incl. Edges)**: '98067A  ' (ISS), '00000   ' (test/unknown).

**Notes**: Often left-padded with spaces. Parse as string; validate format if needed.

---

### Line 1 - Record 5: Epoch (Year)

**Field**: Epoch Year

**Formal Name and Purpose**: Two-digit year of epoch. Used with day-of-year to set reference time.

**Line**: 1

**Columns (1-based, inclusive)**: 19-20

**Width**: 2

**Type**: Integer

**Sign Rules**: Unsigned

**Implied Decimal**: No

**Units**: Years

**Valid Range**: 00–99

**Typical Range**: 57–99 (1957-1999), 00–56 (2000–2056).

**Dependencies/Interactions**: Combined with epoch day to compute Julian date. Windowing: recommend 57-99 -> 19XX, 00-56 -> 20XX.

**Example Values (incl. Edges)**: '98' (1998), '00' (2000), '56' (2056, edge of common window).

**Notes**: Two-digit ambiguity requires windowing. Critical for epoch computation.

---

### Line 1 - Record 6: Epoch (Day of Year)

**Field**: Epoch Day of Year

**Formal Name and Purpose**: Fractional day of year (1.0 = Jan 1 00:00:00). Sets precise epoch time.

**Line**: 1

**Columns (1-based, inclusive)**: 21-32

**Width**: 12

**Type**: Float

**Sign Rules**: Unsigned (always positive)

**Implied Decimal**: Yes, after 3 digits (NNN.NNNNNNNN)

**Units**: Days

**Valid Range**: `1.0 <= value <= 366.99999999` (366 for leap years)

**Typical Range**: 1.0–365.x

**Dependencies/Interactions**: Combined with epoch year to compute Julian date. Validate leap year consistency.

**Example Values (incl. Edges)**: '001.00000000' (Jan 1 00:00:00), '365.99999999' (Dec 31 23:59:59.999), '366.50000000' (leap year, Dec 31 12:00:00).
**Notes**: High precision critical for SGP4. Implied decimal: '00100000000' -> 1.00000000.

---

### Line 1 - Record 7: First Derivative of Mean Motion

**Field**: First Derivative of Mean Motion

**Formal Name and Purpose**: Rate of change of mean motion (dn/dt). Used by SGP4 for drag modeling.

**Line**: 1

**Columns (1-based, inclusive)**: 34-43

**Width**: 10

**Type**: Float

**Sign Rules**: Signed (+ or -)

**Implied Decimal**: Yes, after 2 digits (±N.NNNNNNNN)

**Units**: rev/day²

**Valid Range**: Typically -0.1 to +0.1, but can be larger for high-drag satellites.

**Typical Range**: -0.00001 to +0.00001

**Dependencies/Interactions**: Used by SGP4 for drag perturbations. Often small for stable orbits.

**Example Values (incl. Edges)**: '+.00000123' (slight drag), '-.00012345' (higher drag), '+00000000' (zero, rare).

**Notes**: Leading + often omitted. Implied decimal: '+0000123' → +0.0000123. Critical for SGP4 accuracy.

---

### Line 1 - Record 8: Second Derivative of Mean Motion

**Field**: Second Derivative of Mean Motion

**Formal Name and Purpose**: Second derivative of mean motion (d²n/dt²). Used for advanced drag modeling.

**Line**: 1

**Columns (1-based, inclusive)**: 45-52

**Width**: 8

**Type**: Float (exponential notation)

**Sign Rules**: Signed (+ or -)

**Implied Decimal**: Yes, scientific notation (`±NNNNN±NN → ±0.NNNNNx10^±NN`)

**Units**: rev/day³

**Valid Range**: Typically very small, often zero.

**Typical Range**: -1×10⁻⁵ to +1×10⁻⁵

**Dependencies/Interactions**: Used by SGP4 but often ignored in practice. May be zero for many satellites.

**Example Values (incl. Edges)**: '+12345-4' (+1.2345×10⁻⁴), '-67890-5' (-6.7890×10⁻⁵), '+00000+0' (zero).

**Notes**: Exponential: no 'E'. Parse `±MMMMM±EE` as `±0.MMMMM×10^±EE`. Often zero in public TLEs.

---

### Line 1 - Record 9: B* Drag Term

**Field**: B* Drag Term

**Formal Name and Purpose**: Drag-like parameter (B*). Used by SGP4 for atmospheric drag effects.

**Line**: 1

**Columns (1-based, inclusive)**: 54-61

**Width**: 8

**Type**: Float (exponential notation)

**Sign Rules**: Signed (+ or -)

**Implied Decimal**: Yes, scientific notation (`±NNNNN±NN → ±0.NNNNN×10^±NN`)

**Units**: 1/Earth radii

**Valid Range**: Typically -1 to +1, but can be larger.

**Typical Range**: -0.1 to +0.1

**Dependencies/Interactions**: Critical for SGP4 drag modeling. Satellite-specific based on ballistic coefficient.

**Example Values (incl. Edges)**: '+12345-4' (+1.2345×10⁻⁴), '-67890-3' (-6.7890×10⁻³), '+00000+0' (zero, unlikely).

**Notes**: Same exponential format as second derivative. Essential for accurate SGP4 propagation.

---

### Line 1 - Record 10: Ephemeris Type

**Field**: Ephemeris Type

**Formal Name and Purpose**: Type of ephemeris model. Indicates source/method of orbital determination.

**Line**: 1

**Columns (1-based, inclusive)**: 63-63

**Width**: 1

**Type**: Integer

**Sign Rules**: Unsigned

**Implied Decimal**: No

**Units**: N/A

**Valid Range**: 0–9 (STR#3); 0 is most common for public TLEs.

**Typical Range**: 0 (SGP4-compatible)

**Dependencies/Interactions**: SGP4 designed for type 0. Other types may have different propagation requirements.

**Example Values (incl. Edges)**: '0' (standard SGP4), '1' (alternative, rare), blank (parse as 0).

**Notes**: Public TLEs almost always 0. Parse leniently; warn for non-zero values.

---

### Line 1 - Record 11: Element Set Number

**Field**: Element Set Number

**Formal Name and Purpose**: Sequential number for element set updates. Used to identify latest TLE for a satellite.

**Line**: 1

**Columns (1-based, inclusive)**: 65-68

**Width**: 4

**Type**: Integer

**Sign Rules**: Unsigned

**Implied Decimal**: No

**Units**: N/A

**Valid Range**: 0–9999

**Typical Range**: 1–9999, rolls over at 9999.

**Dependencies/Interactions**: Higher numbers indicate more recent TLEs. Used for versioning, not propagation.

**Example Values (incl. Edges)**: '0001' (first), '9999' (before rollover), '0000' (after rollover).

**Notes**: Right-padded with spaces. Critical for determining most recent TLE.

---

### Line 1 - Record 12: Checksum

**Field**: Checksum

**Formal Name and Purpose**: Modulo-10 checksum for error detection. Validates line integrity.

**Line**: 1

**Columns (1-based, inclusive)**: 69-69

**Width**: 1

**Type**: Integer

**Sign Rules**: Unsigned

**Implied Decimal**: No

**Units**: N/A

**Valid Range**: 0–9

**Typical Range**: 0–9

**Dependencies/Interactions**: Must match computed checksum of columns 1–68.

**Example Values (incl. Edges)**: '0' through '9', any digit.

**Notes**: Checksum algorithm: sum all digits in cols 1–68, where '-'=1, '+'=0, other non-digits=0. Result mod 10.

## Line 2

### Line 2 - Record 1: Line Number

**Field**: Line Number

**Formal Name and Purpose**: Identifies as Line 2. Must be '2' for structural integrity.

**Line**: 2

**Columns (1-based, inclusive)**: 1-1

**Width**: 1

**Type**: Char

**Sign Rules**: N/A

**Implied Decimal**: No

**Units**: N/A

**Valid Range**: '2' only

**Typical Range**: '2'

**Dependencies/Interactions**: Must match '1' on Line 1; mismatch rejects pair.

**Example Values (incl. Edges)**: '2' (normal); blank (reject).

**Notes**: Shall reject if not '2'. Ensures line pairing integrity.

---

### Line 2 - Record 2: Satellite Catalog Number

**Field**: Satellite Catalog Number

**Formal Name and Purpose**: NORAD satellite ID (must match Line 1). Used for line pairing verification.

**Line**: 2

**Columns (1-based, inclusive)**: 3-7

**Width**: 5

**Type**: Integer

**Sign Rules**: Unsigned

**Implied Decimal**: No

**Units**: Unitless

**Valid Range**: 1–99999

**Typical Range**: 1–50000+

**Dependencies/Interactions**: Must match Line 1 exactly. Mismatch rejects the TLE pair.

**Example Values (incl. Edges)**: '00005' (matching Line 1), '99999' (edge case).

**Notes**: Critical for line pairing. Same formatting as Line 1.

---

### Line 2 - Record 3: Inclination

**Field**: Inclination

**Formal Name and Purpose**: Orbital inclination (i). Angle between orbital and equatorial planes.

**Line**: 2

**Columns (1-based, inclusive)**: 9-16

**Width**: 8

**Type**: Float

**Sign Rules**: Unsigned (always positive)

**Implied Decimal**: Yes, after 3 digits (NNN.NNNNN)

**Units**: degrees

**Valid Range**: $0.0 \leq i \leq 180.0$

**Typical Range**: 0°-180°; 0° (equatorial), 90° (polar), > 90° (retrograde)

**Dependencies/Interactions**: Fundamental orbital element for SGP4. Affects satellite ground track.

**Example Values (incl. Edges)**: '000.0000' (equatorial), '090.0000' (polar), '180.0000' (retrograde equatorial).

**Notes**: Implied decimal: '0511234' → 51.1234°. Critical for SGP4 calculations.

---



### Line 2 - Record 4: Right Ascension of Ascending Node

**Field**: Right Ascension of Ascending Node (RAAN)

**Formal Name and Purpose**: Longitude of ascending node (Ω). Defines orbital plane orientation.

**Line**: 2

**Columns (1-based, inclusive)**: 18-25

**Width**: 8

**Type**: Float

**Sign Rules**: Unsigned (always positive, 0–360°)

**Implied Decimal**: Yes, after 3 digits (NNN.NNNNN)

**Units**: degrees

**Valid Range**: $0.0 \leq \Omega < 360.0$

**Typical Range**: 0°–359.9999°

**Dependencies/Interactions**: Fundamental orbital element. Precesses due to Earth oblateness (J₂).

**Example Values (incl. Edges)**: '000.0000' (vernal equinox), '180.0000' (opposite), '359.9999' (near 360°).

**Notes**: Wraps at 360°. Implied decimal: '1801234' → 180.1234°. Essential for orbit plane definition.

---

### Line 2 - Record 5: Eccentricity

**Field**: Eccentricity

**Formal Name and Purpose**: Orbital eccentricity (e). Defines orbit shape (0=circular, < 1=elliptical).

**Line**: 2

**Columns (1-based, inclusive)**: 27-33

**Width**: 7

**Type**: Float

**Sign Rules**: Unsigned (always positive)

**Implied Decimal**: Yes, after 0 digits (`0.NNNNNNN`)

**Units**: dimensionless

**Valid Range**: $0.0 \leq e < 1.0$ (parabolic e = 1 not supported by SGP4)

**Typical Range**: 0.000–0.999; LEO typically $< 0.1$, GEO ~0.0, HEO up to 0.9+

**Dependencies/Interactions**: Fundamental orbital element. High eccentricity requires SDP4 (deep space) mode.

**Example Values (incl. Edges)**: '0000000' (circular), '0012345' (0.012345), '9999999' (0.999999, nearly parabolic).

**Notes**: Always fractional: '1234567' → 0.1234567. No leading zero shown. Critical for orbit shape.

---

### Line 2 - Record 6: Argument of Perigee

**Field**: Argument of Perigee

**Formal Name and Purpose**: Argument of perigee (ω). Angle from ascending node to perigee.

**Line**: 2

**Columns (1-based, inclusive)**: 35-42

**Width**: 8

**Type**: Float

**Sign Rules**: Unsigned (always positive, 0–360°)

**Implied Decimal**: Yes, after 3 digits (NNN.NNNNN)

**Units**: degrees

**Valid Range**: $0.0 \leq \omega < 360.0$

**Typical Range**: 0°–359.9999°

**Dependencies/Interactions**: Fundamental orbital element. For circular orbits (e≈0), the value is meaningless but still parsed.

**Example Values (incl. Edges)**: '000.0000' (perigee at ascending node), '090.0000' (90° past), '270.0000' (270° past).

**Notes**: Wraps at 360°. Implied decimal: '2701234' → 270.1234°. Undefined for circular orbits but still present.

---

### Line 2 - Record 7: Mean Anomaly

**Field**: Mean Anomaly

**Formal Name and Purpose**: Mean anomaly (M). Position along orbit at epoch (0°=perigee, 180°=apogee).

**Line**: 2

**Columns (1-based, inclusive)**: 44-51

**Width**: 8

**Type**: Float

**Sign Rules**: Unsigned (always positive, 0–360°)

**Implied Decimal**: Yes, after 3 digits (NNN.NNNNN)

**Units**: degrees

**Valid Range**: $0.0 \leq M < 360.0$

**Typical Range**: 0°–359.9999°

**Dependencies/Interactions**: Fundamental orbital element. Combined with eccentricity to compute true anomaly.

**Example Values (incl. Edges)**: '000.0000' (at perigee), '180.0000' (at apogee), '090.0000' (90° past perigee).

**Notes**: Wraps at 360°. Implied decimal: '0901234' → 90.1234°. Essential for satellite position at epoch.

---

### Line 2 - Record 8: Mean Motion



**Field**: Mean Motion

**Formal Name and Purpose**: Mean motion (n). Average angular velocity (revolutions per day).

**Line**: 2

**Columns (1-based, inclusive)**: 53-63

**Width**: 11

**Type**: Float

**Sign Rules**: Unsigned (always positive)

**Implied Decimal**: Yes, after 2 digits (NN.NNNNNNNNN)

**Units**: rev/day

**Valid Range**: $> 0.0$; typically 0.1–20.0

**Typical Range**: 1.0-16.0 (LEO: 14-16, GEO: 1.0, HEO: 1.0–7.0)

**Dependencies/Interactions**: Fundamental for SGP4. Determines orbital period. Combined with first derivative for drag effects.

**Example Values (incl. Edges)**: '01.00000000' (1 rev/day, GEO), '15.50000000' (typical LEO), '00.10000000' (high orbit).
**Notes**: High precision essential. Implied decimal: '0150000000' → 1.50000000 rev/day. Defines orbital period.

---

### Line 2 - Record 9: Revolution Number at Epoch

**Field**: Revolution Number at Epoch

**Formal Name and Purpose**: Orbit number at epoch. Used for orbit counting and prediction.

**Line**: 2

**Columns (1-based, inclusive)**: 64-68

**Width**: 5

**Type**: Integer

**Sign Rules**: Unsigned

**Implied Decimal**: No

**Units**: revolutions

**Valid Range**: 0–99999

**Typical Range**: 0–99999, rolls over at 99999

**Dependencies/Interactions**: Combined with mean motion to predict future revolution numbers. Not used by SGP4 for propagation.

**Example Values (incl. Edges)**: '00001' (first orbit), '99999' (before rollover), '00000' (after rollover or initial).

**Notes**: Right-padded with spaces. Used for tracking orbit count, not orbital mechanics.

### Line 2 - Record 10: Checksum

**Field**: Checksum

**Formal Name and Purpose**: Modulo-10 checksum for error detection. Validates line integrity.

**Line**: 2

**Columns (1-based, inclusive)**: 69-69

**Width**: 1

**Type**: Integer

**Sign Rules**: Unsigned

**Implied Decimal**: No

**Units**: N/A

**Valid Range**: 0–9

**Typical Range**: 0–9

**Dependencies/Interactions**: Must match computed checksum of columns 1–68.

**Example Values (incl. Edges)**: '0' through '9', any digit.

**Notes**: Same algorithm as Line 1. Sum all digits in cols 1-68, where '-'=1, '+'=0, other non-digits=0. Result mod 10.
